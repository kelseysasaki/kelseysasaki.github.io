I'm a Leverhulme Trust Early Career Fellow in Linguistics at the University of Oxford. Before this, I did my PhD in Linguistics at UC Santa Cruz.
I am a psycholinguist and experimental semanticist/pragmaticist, specializing in discourse. I also work on exclamatives (e.g., What a cute puppy you have!) and their interactions with clause-embedding predicates. Last but not least, I am a fieldworker with expertise in Santiago Laxopa Zapotec and Hawai'i Creole.
